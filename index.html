THE STORY

In an alternative universe, society is divided into four factions: Veteran (the inventors), conjurer (the wizards), Dauntless (the warriors), and Erudite (the intellectual who rules the society) . And there are the divergents, people who show equal attributes of multiple factions and can think independently. Upon turning 18, teens in this world participate in the Choosing Ceremony and undergo a test to indicate their best-suited faction. Our hero, Colton, he will be participating in the chosing ceremony for this year. And he needs to chose between showing his divergence abilities or hide them.
